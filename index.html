Chicken 65 Wings (Indian Masala Fried Wings)

It’ll blow your mind.

Chicken 65 is a widely popular crispy fried, red hot chicken dish originating in Chennai, India, that is commonly served up as an appetizer or street food. There are a number of theories of how it got it’s name, one of which, is that it gained popularity in 1965. The other that seems quite plausible to me, is that it was featured on a restaurant menu as the 65th dish and was often ordered as “Chicken 65”..
